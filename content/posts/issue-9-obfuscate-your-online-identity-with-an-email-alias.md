<h6 id="the-self-hosting-blog-%E2%80%A2-issue-9">The Self-Hosting Blog • Issue #9</h6><p>Happy Monday!</p><p>In this weeks' issue, I'm going to talk a little about setting up <a href="https://simplelogin.io/">SimpleLogin</a>, we'll be doing a full tutorial post in the future but this is more to make you aware of the service and how it can protect you.</p><p>SimpleLogin allows you to protect your privacy by acting as an anonymising middleman between a service and your inbox. Using SimpleLogin allows you to defend yourself against spam, phishing and data breaches.</p><p>You can self-host SimpleLogin, or used their paid plan to have it managed for you.</p><p>How does it work?</p><p>Using the extension in your browser, when signing up for a new service, you can generate an email address, much like randomly generating a new password. This email address is an alias to your actual email address.</p><p>Any emails that are sent to your auto-generated email get forwarded to your actual email address. You can also reply from your actual email address and the reply will be sent as the auto-generated email address. Therefore always protecting your actual email address.</p><p>All of the aliases that you generate get saved in a dashboard. Getting spammed? Why not disable the alias? no more spam. The spam emails will never make it to your account.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="/images/2021/04/image-2.png" class="kg-image" alt loading="lazy" width="1147" height="754" srcset="/images/size/w600/2021/04/image-2.png 600w, /images/size/w1000/2021/04/image-2.png 1000w, /images/2021/04/image-2.png 1147w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><p>Be sure to checkout SimpleLogin, and look out for our tutorial post in the near future!</p><p>Have a good week!</p><p>Ed</p><hr><h2 id="top-posts-from-our-favourite-bloggers">Top posts from our favourite bloggers</h2><figure class="kg-card kg-bookmark-card"><a class="kg-bookmark-container" href="https://thecrow.uk/mealie-probably-the-best-self-hosted-recipe-manager-in-the-world/"><div class="kg-bookmark-content"><div class="kg-bookmark-title">Mealie - Probably the best self-hosted recipe manager… In the world.</div><div class="kg-bookmark-description">Mealie is a standalone self-hosted recipe manager developed by a mysterious Alaskan known only as Hay Kot.
